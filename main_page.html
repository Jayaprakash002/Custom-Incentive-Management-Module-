Incentive Management System

Welcome to the Incentive Management System designed to streamline your sales performance incentives and holiday package management.

Get Started

Our Goals:

Our system is tailored for a banking organization with approximately 100 employees.

  • Incentive Calculation:
    • Dynamic calculation matrix based on sales performance metrics.
    • Define incentive tiers and percentages according to predefined sales targets.
  • Holiday Package Management:
    • Admin interface for managing holiday packages.
    • Add, edit, and delete holiday packages with comprehensive details.
  • Automated Package Selection:
    • Integrate holiday package selection into the incentive calculation process.
    • Assign holiday packages based on sales performance tiers.
  • Notification System:
    • Email notification system to inform employees of performance and incentive details.
    • Customize email templates for personalized communication.
  • User Access and Permissions:
    • Define user roles and permissions to restrict access to sensitive data.
    • Manage user accounts and permissions for data security.
